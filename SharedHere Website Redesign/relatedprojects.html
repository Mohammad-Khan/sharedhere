iWasHere

iWasHere is an iOS application for sharing and leaving "Drops", which are notes, images, audios and videos, tied to geographic locations for individuals to see. "iWasHere" comes in two versions, a free restricted version and a premium paid version.

GeoMunch

GeoMunch is a location-based reminder application for Android. Users write notes associated with a location, and upon entering the location a notification will be sent out displaying the note. Notes can be associated with any location either by tapping on the map, specifying an address or entering coordinates. It is available as a crippled free version and as a pro version you pay for.
